Personal Biography

Hi, my name is Gracie and I am a student at the University of Maryland, studying multi-platform journalism under the Philip Merrill College of Journalism. I am also pursuing a minor in public leadership. I'm from Frederick, Maryland.

While attending the University of Maryland, I have worked as the Editor-in-Chief, President and Social Media Director of the publication Her Campus Maryland. Under my leadership, our publication has produced a plethora of stories written by women, for women. Her Campus Maryland publishes articles weekly, creates social media content, and meets with women of diverse backgrounds in order to establish a unique, online women’s magazine. We provide news, feature and opinion pieces engulfed in the topics of women’s empowerment, feminist politics, diverse entertainment and many others.

Additionally, I have worked as a reporting intern for Montgomery Community Media, which has allowed me to expand my experience of reporting on deadline and finding localized angles for stories. Working as an intern has opened my eyes to how easy it is to make a mistake in journalism, as well as how to avoid such mistakes. I’ve gained significant experience with reporting stories, editing photographs, creating social media content and copy editing others’ work.

Outside of journalism, some of my hobbies include exercising, yoga, running and reading. On campus, I'm involved in a variety of organizations and am a part of the University Honors Living and Learning program.
